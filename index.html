Deforestation

Fact 1

Deforestation can result in more carbon dioxide being released into the atmosphere. The most amount of deforestation is happening in tropical rainforests, which is leading to beautiful and natural rainforests being polluted and desperate for a better environment.

Fact 2

Lots of farmers every year cause a lot of deforestation because they clear trees to create space, and then planting the same seeds causing biodiversity loss.

Fact 3

Deforestation also causes habitat loss. Trees are a crucial part of animals' homes, for example; birds use trees to build their nests, raccoons use tree hollows to keep them dry on rainy days and cool on hot days and even leaves that are often laid on by ladybugs, grasshoppers etc.
